Limit time as well as day choice
  1. Click on the '...' button to display the calendar. Notice that you can choose the time at the bottom of the calendar.
  2. You cannot choose Sunday
  3. Choose any Friday of the month.
  4. Notice that on Fridays you cannot choose times less than 08:00 and greater then 21:45
  5. On Saturday you can only choose times between 11:00 and 17:00
  6. Choose any day of the month except Friday, Saturday or Sunday.
  7. Notice that you can choose any time you want.
  8. Choose 23:00, and change the day to a Satuday. Notice that the time automatically scrolls past 11:00 since that's the first valid time for that day.
